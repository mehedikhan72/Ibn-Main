Why IBNMAIN?

Sustainable Sourcing

We take a responsible approach to sourcing our ingredients, ensuring that they are ethically and sustainably harvested. Our ouds are made with rare and precious materials that are carefully selected to protect the environment and support local communities.

Artisanal Blends

Our ouds are created using traditional methods and infused with exotic ingredients from around the world. We work with skilled artisans who blend and distill the oils in small batches to ensure each bottle of perfume is a work of art.

Personalized Experience

We believe that scent is a deeply personal experience, which is why we offer a range of customization options to help our customers find their perfect fragrance. From personalized consultations with our expert perfumers to bespoke bottle designs, we strive to create a one-of-a-kind experience for each and every customer.

About Ibn Main

The start of the Oud business founded by two siblings recently is a story of hope and determination. As they embarked on this journey, the siblings were filled with excitement, yet nervousness about what was to come. The idea of building a business from scratch seemed daunting, but they were ready to face the challenges ahead

Starting a business is never an easy task, but for these siblings, it was more than just a business. It was a dream that they had nurtured for years, a passion that they had shared since childhood. They had always been fascinated by the sweet aroma of Oud, and the idea of sharing that with the world was something that gave them immense joy.
